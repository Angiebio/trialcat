A Real Cat AI Labs Simulation

Race to Approval

A strategy sim where you're the sponsor and the contagion is your own clinical program. Shepherd a real FDA-regulated product from first-in-human to approval before the money, the clock, or the competition buries it. Can you run your trials fast enough?

🎲 Real data, real stakes

Every game deals you an actual trial from ClinicalTrials.gov. A drug routes you through IND → phases → NDA/BLA. A device forks to 510(k) or PMA. The board knows the difference.

🧪 Spend wisely

Capital, reputation, evidence, and time. Enroll fast and dirty, or slow and clean. Engage the FDA early, or roll the dice. Every choice burns a resource you'll miss later.

🗂️ Face the reviewer

At submission, make your case to Dr. Eleanor Vance of the Division of Regulatory Reckoning. She has read everything. She is not impressed. Her highest praise is "this is adequate." Convince her anyway.
